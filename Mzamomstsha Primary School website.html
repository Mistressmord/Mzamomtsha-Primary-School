Mzamomtsha Primary School

Who Are We?

Mzamomtsha is a school situated in Driftsands street Mfuleni, Khayelitsha that has number of strong teaches and learners who are all keen in growing their ability to teach and to learn.

Our Vision

Our vision here at Mzamomtsha Primary school is to empower more students to value their knowledge and skills that will support them to participate in, to contribute to the world as a whole and to practice the core values of our school which are RESPECT, TOLERANCE, INCLUSION and EXCELLENCE.

Our Mision

Our mission here is mainly to develop students with an active and creative mind to a sense of understanding and compassion for others. To have courage to act on their beliefs.
